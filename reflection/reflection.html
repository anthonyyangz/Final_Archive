First of all, the first thing that I felt throughout the whole course is efficiency on learning the language for website and computer. This course is useful for me since my major is Architecture and I need to learn a lot of things related to computer-based design. I found myself to learn actually quite a lot from the demos from online resources. However, if this class has a more advanced level in the future, I would love to take it again.
